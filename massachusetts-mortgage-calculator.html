Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average mortgage rate in Massachusetts?

As of 2026, average Massachusetts mortgage rates range from 6.3% to 6.9% for a 30-year fixed-rate loan.

How much down payment do I need in Massachusetts?

Most lenders require 3-20% down. Conventional loans need 5-20%, FHA loans accept as little as 3.5%.

Are property taxes high in Massachusetts?

Massachusetts has an effective property tax rate of approximately 1.23% annually.

What is PMI?

PMI is required if down payment is less than 20%. Once you reach 20% equity, you can request cancellation.

What documents do I need for a Massachusetts mortgage?

Pay stubs, W-2s or tax returns (2 years), bank statements (3 months), valid ID, and debt details.

How long does mortgage approval take in Massachusetts?

Mortgage approval in Massachusetts typically takes 30-45 days from application to closing.
