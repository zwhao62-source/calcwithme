Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average mortgage rate in Maryland?

As of 2026, average Maryland mortgage rates range from 6.2% to 6.8% for a 30-year fixed-rate loan.

How much down payment do I need in Maryland?

Most lenders require 3-20% down. Conventional loans need 5-20%, FHA loans accept as little as 3.5%.

Are property taxes high in Maryland?

Maryland has an effective property tax rate of approximately 1.09% annually.

What is PMI?

PMI is required if down payment is less than 20%. Once you reach 20% equity, you can request cancellation.

What documents do I need for a Maryland mortgage?

Pay stubs, W-2s or tax returns (2 years), bank statements (3 months), valid ID, and debt details.

How long does mortgage approval take in Maryland?

Mortgage approval in Maryland typically takes 30-45 days from application to closing.
