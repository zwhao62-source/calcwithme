Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average mortgage rate in New York?

As of 2026, average New York mortgage rates range from 6.4% to 7.0% for a 30-year fixed-rate loan.

How much down payment do I need in New York?

Most lenders require 3-20% down. Conventional loans need 5-20%, FHA loans accept as little as 3.5%.

Are property taxes high in New York?

New York has an effective property tax rate of approximately 1.69% annually.

What is PMI?

PMI is required if down payment is less than 20%. Once you reach 20% equity, you can request cancellation.

What documents do I need for a New York mortgage?

Pay stubs, W-2s or tax returns (2 years), bank statements (3 months), valid ID, and debt details.

How long does mortgage approval take in New York?

Mortgage approval in New York typically takes 30-45 days from application to closing.
