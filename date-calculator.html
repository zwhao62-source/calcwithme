Date Calculator

Calculate the number of days between two dates, add or subtract days from any date, or find what date falls a certain number of days from today. This tool handles leap years automatically and includes all calendar nuances, making it perfect for project planning, contract deadlines, pregnancy calculations, and more.

How Days Between Dates Are Calculated

The calculator counts every calendar day between your start and end dates. When calculating the difference, it uses the Gregorian calendar which has 365 days per year (366 in leap years). The calculation subtracts the start date from the end date and returns the total number of days. Note: if both dates are the same, the result is 0 days.

Business Days vs Calendar Days

Calendar days count every day including weekends and holidays. Business days (working days) exclude Saturdays, Sundays, and public holidays. If you need to calculate business days, look for a business days calculator or manually exclude weekends from the total.

Common Uses

  • Calculate how many days until a vacation or event
  • Determine your due date (pregnancy is 280 days from last menstrual period)
  • Count days between project milestones
  • Calculate warranty or subscription expiration dates
  • Find out what day of the week a future date falls on
