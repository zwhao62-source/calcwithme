Wyoming Home Insurance Overview

Wyoming homeowners insurance averages $2,080/year ($173/month), which is near the national average of $2,230/year. Wyoming rates are moderate compared to the national average.

  • Average annual premium: $2,080
  • Average monthly: $173
  • Per $1,000 coverage: $7.84
  • Median home value: $265,300
  • Wyoming rank: #25 most expensive state

What Wyoming Home Insurance Covers

  • Dwelling: Physical structure of your home and attached structures
  • Other structures: Detached garage, shed, fence (typically 10% of dwelling coverage)
  • Personal property: Furniture, electronics, clothing (50-70% of dwelling)
  • Loss of use: Hotel and living expenses if home is uninhabitable (20-30% of dwelling)
  • Liability: Legal protection if someone is injured on your property ($100K-$300K standard)
  • Medical payments: Small medical bills for guests injured on your property ($1K-$5K)

Wyoming-Specific Insurance Risks

  • Severe weather: Thunderstorms, wind, and hail are the most common claims
  • Winter storms: Pipe bursts and ice dams in colder months
  • Water damage: Most common claim type nationally — check plumbing coverage

How to Save on Wyoming Home Insurance

  • Bundle home + auto: Save 10-25% on both policies with the same insurer
  • Raise your deductible: Going from $500 to $2,000 deductible can save 15-25%
  • Improve home security: Alarm systems, deadbolts, and smoke detectors save 5-15%
  • Maintain good credit: In most states, credit score impacts insurance rates significantly
  • Stay claim-free: 3-5 years without a claim earns a 10-20% discount
  • Shop every 2 years: Rates change constantly. Get 3-5 quotes each renewal cycle
  • Ask about Wyoming discounts: New roof, impact-resistant roofing, wind mitigation, loyalty

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is home insurance in Wyoming?

Average home insurance in Wyoming costs $2,080/year or $173/month. That's $7.84 per $1,000 of coverage. Rates vary by location, home age, and coverage level.

Is home insurance required in Wyoming?

Wyoming law does not require homeowners insurance, but your mortgage lender will require it. Even without a mortgage, insurance protects your largest asset from fire, storms, theft, and liability.
