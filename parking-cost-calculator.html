Understanding Parking Costs

Parking can be a significant hidden cost of car ownership, especially in urban areas. Smart parking strategies can save you hundreds of dollars per year.

Average Parking Costs by City Type

  • Small Cities: $40-80/month
  • Medium Cities: $100-150/month
  • Large Metros: $175-250/month
  • Premium Downtown: $300-500+/month

Ways to Save on Parking

  • Look for monthly passes (often 20-40% cheaper than daily rates)
  • Consider public transit for commuting
  • Use apps like SpotHero or ParkMobile to find deals
  • Park further away and walk (often saves money + exercise)
  • Explore car-sharing instead of owning a car
